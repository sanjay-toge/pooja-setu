## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register and use PoojaSetu, we collect the following personal information:

- **Name:** Your full name for identification and booking purposes
- **Email Address:** For account verification, notifications, and communication
- **Phone Number:** For account authentication and booking confirmations
- **Profile Photo:** Optional profile picture uploaded by you
- **Date of Birth (DOB):** For personalized spiritual services
- **Place of Birth (POB):** For astrological and spiritual service requirements
- **Spiritual Details:** Including Gotra, Nakshatra, and intentions for pooja rituals

### 2.2 Booking and Transaction Information

- Payment information and transaction amounts (in INR)
- Add-on services selected
- Booking status and history

### 2.3 Authentication Information

- Authentication provider information (Phone, Google, or Facebook)
- Authentication tokens and session data
- Login timestamps and activity logs

### 2.4 Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** App usage patterns, features accessed, time spent on the App
- **Log Data:** IP address, crash reports, performance data
- **Location Data:** Approximate location based on IP address (not precise GPS location)

### 2.5 Media and Communications

- **Live Stream Access:** Metadata about live pooja streams you view
- **Recorded Videos:** URLs and access logs for recorded pooja ceremonies
- **Camera/Photos:** Only if you choose to upload a profile photo (optional)
